Our Mission

TrueImage Labs' goal is to provide tools for everyone to help detect synthetic images and for researchers to learn about different techniques for detecting synthetic images. 

We believe arming people with tools and knowledge is the best way to combat harmful uses of synthetically generated images.

Our Next Steps

Public Testing of Synthetic Image Detectors

We plan to offer 2 more models based on 2 different papers that detect Stable-Diffusion generated images by July 

Blogging

Documentation and articles by researchers explaining the uses and dangers of Synthetic images will be available this August. 

We also look forward to publishing original research by researcher soon
