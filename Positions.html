We're Hiring!

All positions are volunteer positions. We have flexible hours and operate asynchronously

Machine Learning Specialist

High Demand

TrueImage Labs is currently working on introducing more methods to detect synthetically generated image and specialized versions of our current synthetic image detection models. You will be responsible for finding papers on methods to detect synthetic images, reading and ​implementing methods described in papers, and writing short summaries.  

Preferred Qualifications: ​Undergraduate or higher pursuing a degree in CS/CE/Math or equivalent; Prior ​experience with image classification projects and Pytorch.

Researcher - Technical Writing

High Demand

TrueImage Labs aims to educate the general public on AI-generated images and media. We are looking for people who enjoy writing and are passionate about teaching. You will be responsible for conducting original research, reviewing papers, drawing simple diagrams, writing short and concise articles. 

Preferred Qualifications: ​Experience with writing reviews on research papers; Prior exposure to basic terminology in machine learning and computer vision; 

Frontend Developer

High Demand

TrueImage Labs aims to provide synthetic image detection tools that are easy and intuitive to use. Additionally, we hope to expand the availability of our detection tools by introducing Progressive Web Application(PWA) version and a browser extension that checks for synthetically generated image within a website.

Depending on your prior ​experience, you may be responsible for 

  • Formatting certain section of our website & UI designing
  • Making our website PWA compliant
  • Prototyping browser extensions

Preferred Qualifications: ​​Experience with Javascript, CSS, and HTML; ​Experience in web-development projects;  

Recruiter 

Moderate Demand

TrueImage Labs hopes to recruit a talented and passionate team that is passionate about AI-saftey. You will be responsible for reaching out to various undergraduate communities, assisting with the onboarding process, motivating team members, and answering inquiries. 

Preferred Qualifications: ​Experience with persuasive writing and ​advertising; Leadership ​experience

Outreach Manager

Moderate Demand

TrueImage Labs hopes to bring our synthetic image detectors to platforms beyond our app and website. Our next major goal is to collaborate with image hosting websites and social media platforms to bring our synthetic image detectors to much larger user bases. You will be responsible for writing convincing emails; reaching out to various organizations and companies; and managing our social media platforms.

Preferred Qualifications: ​Experience with persuasive writing and ​advertising; Leadership ​experience

Backend-developer

Low Demand

TrueImage Labs hosts many synthetic image detection tools. We need a robust and efficient backend server. You will be responsible for ​maintaining our server, implementing various utility tools, and testing our website. 

Preferred Qualifications: ​Experience with ​verifying program correctness; Finding and reading documentation; 

General Advice

  • Regardless of what area you plan contributing in, good communication and taking initiative are key ​characteristics to your success at TrueImage Labs
  • In you application, Include projects(websites, programs, writings, or videos) that demonstrate your expertise in the area you plan on contributing in.
  • We don't expect you to be proficient in every area you plan on contributing in; Lots of learning occurs during your time at TrueImage Labs!
  • We are looking for candidates who take initiative and have leadership skills to take on leadership positions.
  • Don't hesitate to reach out. Feel free to email contact@trueimagelabs.org with any questions or concerns.

  • We are currently looking to recruit an experienced software developer who can oversee and mentor several undergraduates as Vice President of Software ​Engineering; and a machine learning researcher who will oversee several undergraduates researchers as Vice President of Machine Learning.                                         

Contact us if you are interested in either of these positions.
